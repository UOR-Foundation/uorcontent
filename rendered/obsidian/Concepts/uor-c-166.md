# Axiom Canonical Representation Statement

## Description

The formal statement of the [[uor-c-004|Canonical Representation]] Axiom: The image Ï†(X) is a unique, lossless encoding that does not depend on numeral base or observer frame and achieves the minimal [[uor-c-005|coherence norm]] among all representations.

## Mathematical Formulation

$
\phi(X) \text{ is unique, lossless, base-independent, and frame-invariant}
$

$
\forall R \text{ (alternative representation)}, \|\phi(X)\| \leq \|R(X)\|
$
